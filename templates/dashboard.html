About Group31

Group31 is dedicated to combating phishing attacks and enhancing Gmail security. Our mission is to provide users with a robust tool that intelligently detects and neutralizes phishing threats, ensuring a safer digital environment for everyone.

With the rise of sophisticated phishing techniques, protecting your personal and professional information has never been more crucial. Group31 leverages advanced technology to offer real-time scanning, AI-powered detection, and proactive alerts to keep you one step ahead of cybercriminals.

Phishing Advice

Always Check Sender

Verify the sender's email address. Phishers often use addresses that look similar to legitimate ones but have subtle differences.

Hover Before You Click

Before clicking any link, hover over it to see the actual URL. Malicious links might direct you to fake websites.

Beware of Urgency

Phishing emails often create a sense of urgency or threat to pressure you into immediate action. Take a moment to think.

Protect Your Credentials

Never share your passwords, PINs, or other sensitive information via email or unsecured forms.
