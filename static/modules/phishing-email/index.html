What is Phishing Email?

Phishing is a social engineering attack often used to steal user data. Attackers disguise as a trustworthy entity to dupe victims into opening emails, messages, or links.

Common Tactics:

  • Urgent/threatening language
  • Links to fake login pages
  • Generic greetings
  • Malicious attachments
  • Spoofed sender addresses

Prevention Strategies:

  • Verify sender address
  • Hover on links before clicking
  • Be suspicious of urgency
  • Never provide credentials by email
  • Use multi-factor authentication
