Understanding Tailgating Attacks
Learn about this common physical security threat and how to prevent it

What is Tailgating?

Tailgating (or "piggybacking") is when an unauthorized person follows an authorized person into a restricted area without proper authentication.

Common Tactics:

  • Carrying items to appear busy/legitimate
  • Wearing uniforms or business attire
  • Creating urgency or time pressure
  • Claiming to be new employees or contractors

Prevention Strategies:

  • Always require proper authentication
  • Don't hold doors open for strangers
  • Verify identity through official channels
  • Report suspicious behavior to security
  • Follow company security policies
  • Trust your instincts if something feels wrong
