Learn to recognize and defend against pretexting social engineering attacks.

Red Flags in Verification Requests:

  • Urgent language and time pressure
  • Requests for sensitive information via email/phone
  • Threats of account suspension or legal action
  • Impersonation of authority figures or IT staff
  • Requests for passwords or security codes

How to Protect Yourself:

  • Verify the requester through official channels
  • Never provide sensitive info via email or phone
  • Take time to think - don't rush decisions
  • Contact the organization directly using known numbers
  • Trust your instincts if something feels wrong
